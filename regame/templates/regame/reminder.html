Quick reminder

  • \w is any letter, digit or underscore.
  • \W is anything but letter, digit or underscore.
  • \d is any digit.
  • Characters in [brackets] is one of those characters. Dash means range eg. [a-z] is lowercase letter.
  • (foo|bar) is one of foo or bar
  • {number} is that many repetition of previous pattern.
  • {number,} is at least that many repetition.
